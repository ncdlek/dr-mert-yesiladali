Uterine fibroids are benign tumors. Many are asymptomatic.

## Symptoms
- Heavy bleeding, pain, frequency

## Diagnosis
- Exam, ultrasound, MRI when needed

## Treatment
- Watchful waiting, medication, minimally invasive/open surgery, embolization
